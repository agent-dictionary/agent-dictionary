# Evaluation and Safety

Evaluation and safety terms describe how agent systems are tested, constrained,
observed, and corrected.

## Terms

- [Eval](../terms/eval.md)
- [Guardrail](../terms/guardrail.md)
- [Human-in-the-Loop](../terms/human-in-the-loop.md)
- [Trace](../terms/trace.md)
- [Autonomy](../terms/autonomy.md)

## Useful Distinction

An eval measures behavior. A guardrail tries to constrain behavior. A trace
helps explain what happened after behavior occurs.
